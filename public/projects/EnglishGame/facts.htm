Instructions
  • A characters speed is how far they can move per roll of the die. For example Speed: +2 means that you will move +2 spaces per roll. If you rolled a 4 then you will move 4+2= 6 spaces. If you roll a 3 you will move 5 spaces.
  • Money: 25$ means you will recieve 25 dollars/points per space. If you move 10 spaces you'll recieve 250 dollars (10x25).
  • Now luck is simply your chances at getting a good card when you land on "draw a card." Luck has nothing to do with rolling the die. If you choose Grendel, your luck will be quite poor. You'll find that quite often you recieve cards that are negative in some way.
  • Finally, each character is different. Each one comes with his/her own story. Example: One of Grendels' cards will say "Beowulf has taken your arm" and you will lose money or go back a number of spaces.
